About us

who are we

Ecopharm is an established and rapidly growing retail pharmacy chain in Uganda where top-notch professionals dedicate time and skills to care for patients and other shoppers.

We boast of 19 physical stores around Kampala, Entebbe and Wakiso and are still growing. We are different both structurally and culturally from other typical pharmacies you will find. The attitude here is collaborative, family-like and not cut-throat. Decisions are driven by scientific evidence and data. Brilliance and growth are supported, celebrated and rewarded.

our mission

To be the leading provider of quality pharmaceutical care

our vision

To provide quality products at the most affordable price and the best customer experience.

               

About lourite phamarcy

We have positioned ourselves as consistent providers of quality and dependable pharmaceutical products at very affordable prices ensuring a professional distribution and supply infrastructure that you can trust!

C&A is an established entity focusing on selling medicines and other various items such as baby care products, toiletries and cosmetics since 1997. We strive to give quality pharmaceutical care and excellent customer service while offering cost effective medicinal drugs taking care to respect the customer’s confidentiality.

It is our strong belief that the right care and service must be given to everyone who pays us a visit, whether they are waiting for their prescriptions, buying toiletries, baby care products, cosmetics or just passing by.

We have 4 branches in Kampala with convenient operating hours and our locations are strategically positioned to suit our customer’s convenience.
